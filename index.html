Substitution Cipher

The Substitution Cipher requires a standard alphabet and a substitution alphabet of 26 characters. Letters from the standard alphabet will be transposed to the substitution alphabet. This cipher requires that the recipient have the substitution alphabet, otherwise it will be difficult for them to decode the message.
